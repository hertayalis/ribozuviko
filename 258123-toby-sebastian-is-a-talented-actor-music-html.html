"The Music of Silence" actor Toby Sebastian is the older brother of little women actress Florence Pugh. Find out more about his acting and music career and his relationship with his famous younger sister.

Actress Florence Pugh's brother Toby Sebastian is a charming multitalented actor and musician. Learn more about the actress's older brother.

WHO IS TOBY SEBASTIAN?

Actor Toby Sebastian is most popularly known for his role in the HBO series "Game of Thrones" and "The Music Of Silence." The British actor is also a musician, and before he came into the limelight as an actor, he worked tirelessly to build his music career.

Florence Pugh at the 2020 Vanity Fair Oscar party on February 09, 2020 in Beverly Hills, California | Photo: Getty Images

Everyone loves a multitalented man, and Sebastian is in no shortage whatsoever of talents. In 2020, while the entire world had its eyes on his younger sister, Florence Pugh, the dark-haired charmer quickly stole the spotlight when the internet discovered him.

A Twitter user who seemingly just found out that Sebastian is Pugh's brother took to the platform with a particularly dashing photo of him. She then wrote:

"ladies and gentlemen.......... Florence Pugh's brother."

Garnering over fifty thousand likes and several thousand retweets and comments, the photo of the actor/musician traveled like wildfire on the platform. The actor became a sensation overnight!

HIS CAREER

Sebastian reportedly first appeared on television in 2008 as a contestant on "Orange unsignedAct." Four years later, he got his first acting gig in the movie "After The dark."

It was in 2015 that he finally appeared in the fifth season of "Game Of Thrones." He played Trystane Martell, the youngest child of the series' Prince of Dorne, Doran Martell.

Although Toby Sebastian's "Game of Thrones" journey only lasted for six episodes, he certainly made his mark. Come 2017, he appeared in the Italian biographical film "The Music Of Silence" about Italian tenor Andrea Bocelli's childhood and singing career.

Sebastian opened up about his excitement and anxiety about playing the famous singer. After he got to meet with Bocelli himself, Sebastian said to Hollywood Life:

"I started filming...and I basically watched him sing those songs to me and I thought, "Wow, have I bitten off more than I can chew?"

Of course, Sebastian went on to crush the role and did incredibly well. Fans can also get a taste of Toby Sebastian's songs as he released an EP titled Hamliar produced by Matthew Benbrook in 2019.

HIS SISTER, FLORENCE PUGH

Actress Florence Pugh has very quickly found her wave to prominence in the acting industry after years of hard work and determination. Since her professional debut in the 2014 mystery film "The Falling," she has since garnered many awards and great roles under her belt.

Best known for her roles in 2016's "Lady Macbeth," 2019's "Fighting With My Family," and "Little Women," Pugh has proved that she has an impressively blooming future awaiting her.

THEIR FAMILY

Born Sebastian Toby M. Pugh, the 28-year-old Englishman was born in Oxford, England. However, he spent most of his early years in Andalusia, Spain, with his sisters, Florence, Arabella, and Rafaela.
