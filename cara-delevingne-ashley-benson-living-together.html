  • Cara Delevingne and Ashley Benson have reportedly taken their relationship to the next level by moving in together.
  • Us Weekly is reporting that Benson sold her house in Los Angeles and moved into Delevingne's.

Ashley Benson and Cara Delevingne's relationship is apparently getting more serious.

According to Us Weekly, Delevingne and Benson have reportedly moved in together. Per the outlet's source, Benson is said to have "sold her house in Los Angeles" and "moved into Cara’s place there."

While Delevingne and Benson remain extremely private about their relationship, they have been spotted together more frequently in recent weeks. For instance, they were seen carrying a sex bench in Los Angeles, they've been photographed kissing in public, and they've even cozied up on Instagram.
