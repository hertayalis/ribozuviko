Emma Myers Movies, TV Shows, and Awards

Emma embarked on her acting journey in 2010, playing a limited role in the drama show “Letters to God.” Later that year, she was also cast to play Paige Slayton, a supporting character in the award-winning action-drama show “The Glades.”

In 2021, the actress received early recognition for portraying the impressive role of Marie Cody in the crime-thriller movie “Girl in the Basement,” which received positive reviews from both audiences and critics.

The following year, she rose to global prominence by playing the lead role of Enid Sinclair alongside Jenna Ortega in Netflix’s horror-fantasy series “Wednesday.” The show was both commercially as well as critically successful and won the Kid’s Choice Awards for favorite family TV show.

In addition, Emma’s commendable performance in the show won her a nomination for Best Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series at the OFTA Television award.

Besides this, the actress has performed notable characters in numerous other successful movies and television shows, including Family Switch (2023), Southern Gospel (2023), A Taste of Christmas (2020), Dead of Night (2020), Deathless (2020), Love, Innocence (2019), and Crooked (2010), among others.

Moving ahead, Emma has also gained a massive fan following on social media platforms. She is mainly active on Instagram, where she has over 11.2 million followers, and mainly shares her lifestyle photos.

With her growing fame on the platform, a few fashion and makeup brands have signed her as their official digital face. Furthermore, she has also been featured on the covers of several fashion magazines, including Harper’s Bazaar, Cosmopolitan, and others.
