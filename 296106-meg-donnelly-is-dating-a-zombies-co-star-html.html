Meg Donnelly Is Dating a 'Zombies' Co-star

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Meg Donnelly | Meg Donnelly and Noah Zulfikar | Source: Instagram.com/megdonnelly | Source: Instagram.com/noah_zul

Meg Donnelly has been linked to two of her "Z-O-M-B-I-E-S" co-stars. However, she has only been public about her relationship with Noah Zulfikar, who sometimes appears on her Instagram posts.

Since launching into stardom, Meg Donnelly's dating life has become of public interest. The New York City-born actress was only six years old when she began training for a career in the entertainment industry.

Although still very young, her personal life has already attracted the attention of fans. If not rumored to be in a relationship with a man she is seen with, Donnelly is linked to a co-star.

The actress has since starred in several on-screen productions, including "Team Toon," "Future Shock," "What Would You Do?" "American Housewife," and "Z-O-M-B-I-E-S," where she was linked with her co-star, Milo Manheim.

Who Is Meg Donnelly Dating Now?

Donnelly's last known relationship was with Noah Zulfikar. Although their relationship timeline is unknown, the actress reportedly confirmed their romance during an Instagram Live. She also paid tribute to Zulfikar on his birthday for the past three years.

In 2020, she shared a snap of sitting beside Zulfikar with both legs across his thigh while leaning towards him. "You are the brightest light," she said. "Thank you for showing me what love truly is."

Two years later, she posted a series of photos and videos of her boyfriend being affectionate, silly, and sweet. Some of the videos showed the couple jamming to songs and Zulfikar exhibiting his rapping skills. The actor also shared some snaps with his girlfriend on his Instagram for her birthday.

Noah Zulfikar

Born in Ontario, Canada, Zulfikar is a trained dancer who knows contemporary, ballet, hip-hop, tap, jazz, and musical theater. Mentored under the Canadian Dance Company, he has proven to be skillful in his craft, winning competitions in America.

At the young age of 16, Zulfikar launched his career and landed a role on the hit television show "The Next Step." He is still at the onset of his fame in the entertainment industry, but with his talent, more opportunities may come.

Who Else Was Meg Donnelly Dating?

Besides Zulfikar, Donnelly was linked to her other Disney Channel co-star, Milo Manheim. The duo met during the "Z-O-M-B-I-E-S" audition and ended up getting to know each other. After singing a duet, their friendship formed.

According to Donnelly, she and Manheim share a "special bond." The actors have nothing but kind words to say about each other, prompting fans to believe that they are more than just friends.

Manheim addressed the rumors and assured fans that their relationship is nothing more than a strong friendship. However, he understands where the speculation came from. "I get it. We're perfect. So it makes sense," he said.

He has also formed close bonds with his other castmates, who he considers family. Manheim considers himself lucky to have a great group of actors on the show, both on-screen and in real life.

Besides his friends-turned-family, Manheim has gained a lot from playing Zed on the show. He claimed that his character taught him how to go after his passion and be his own person.

The young star has shown signs of becoming an entertainer at an early age, even participating in shows at age six.

Manheim is the son of “Law & Order” star, Camryn Manheim. Although he has the constant guidance of his mom to teach him the ropes in the industry, it is Manheim's talent and hard work that got him to where he is.
