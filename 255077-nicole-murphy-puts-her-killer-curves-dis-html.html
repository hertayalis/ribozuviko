Nicole Murphy Puts Her Killer Curves on Display Posing on a Yacht in a Stylish White Swimsuit

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Eddie Murphy’s ex-wife, Nicole Murphy, took to Instagram to show off her curves in a white swimsuit while enjoying a good time on a yacht.

Even at 53, American model Nicole Murphy sure has her way with a one-piece. The star shared a picture of herself on Instagram, looking hot and glamorous with her curves in full display as she posed on a yacht.

The photo showed the mother-of-five dressed in a stylish white one-piece swimsuit which matched the exteriors of the lavish yacht.

Nicole Mitchell Murphy attends the 28th Annual Talk of the Town Gala on November 18, 2017 | Photo: Getty Images

Aside from the breathtaking white fabric, the swimsuit looked extraordinary, forming an “X” that extended down her hips, revealing portions of her ribs, abdomen, and generous cleavage.

Completing the ravishing look was the raffia sun hat secured firmly with a drawstring underneath the model’s neck. Murphy appeared excited while posing with both hands on the stylish hat and her face looking towards the dreamy clouds. She wrote:

"Think like a queen. A queen is not afraid to fail. Failure is another stepping stone to greatness."

Coming from a woman who experienced her bout of failure years ago following her 2005 split from her ex-husband Eddie Murphy and emerging stronger, those words meant everything.

Five years after divorcing the "Nutty Professor" star, the model opened up about one of her biggest brushes with failures. She admitted to being broke, saying she lost most of her assets and divorce entitlements to a fraudster.

Murphy has garnered an impressive number of subscribers to her fitness routine

The 53-year-old also descended into debts, owing several people including a landscaping firm and a law firm. She also had an outstanding payment of up to $5 million on her Los Angeles apartment.

Eventually, the actress found a way to recover from her lowest point, emerging on the other side. She embraced some healthy lifestyle changes which she tapped into in the long run as a means of growing her income. Murphy once explained:

"I think with age, you build your self-esteem, you become more confident. I'm confident within myself. I love to embrace my age."

Over the years, Murphy has become adherent to her daily workout and diet routines, which helped her achieve the perfect figure. Through her fitness website, the icon shares several secrets to maintaining a healthy body.

In addition to her other financial projects, Murphy has garnered an impressive number of subscribers to her fitness routine. That way, she remains in shape while profiting from the venture financially.

Little wonder the mother of five still maintains the picture-perfect curves of a teenager even at 53! It is obvious she has really done well for herself!
