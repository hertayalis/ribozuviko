Owain Yeoman is a Welsh actor. He first debuted in the 2004 film Troy, as well as had his first TV role in Midsomer Murders. At the same time, he had performances in the films Chromeskull: Laid to Rest 2 and American Sniper. He went on to star in the films The Belko Experiment and Brahms: The Boy II.
