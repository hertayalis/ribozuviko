She then pursued a career in modeling before returning to acting in 2006 with the comedy film “Filthy Gorgeous.” In 2008, she appeared in successful roles in the horror film “The Ruins” and the thriller film “Stiletto.” Additionally, she portrayed Lola in the TV comedy series “The Starter Wife.”

In 2013, Bar gained recognition for her role as Sorina Luminita in Michael Bay’s action film “Pain & Gain.” Subsequently, she appeared in the sports drama “Million Dollar Arm” in 2014. Her most prominent role came in 2015 when she was cast as Anna Kolcheck in the TV crime-drama series “NCIS: Los Angeles,” appearing in 13 episodes.

Bar’s career continued successfully, and in 2016, she played Denise in the thriller film “Urge.” She also portrayed Krisztina in the TV series “Jean-Claude Van Johnson.” She is currently working on the thriller film “Headlock,” scheduled for release later this year.
