10 Fun Things to Do in Jonesboro

July 2024 · 4 minute read

When it comes to finding enjoyable activities, Jonesboro has something for everyone. This vibrant city in Arkansas offers a wide range of attractions and experiences that are sure to delight locals and tourists alike. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, history buff, or simply looking for some family fun, Jonesboro has got you covered. In this article, we’ll explore 10 exciting things to do in Jonesboro that will make your visit a memorable one.

1. Explore Nature at Craighead Forest Park

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Jonesboro at Craighead Forest Park. This sprawling park offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking trails, fishing spots, and picnic areas. Rent a paddleboat or canoe and explore the serene lake, or challenge yourself with a game of disc golf.

2. Visit the Arkansas State University Museum

Delve into the rich history and culture of Jonesboro at the Arkansas State University Museum. With its extensive collection of artifacts and exhibits, this museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the region’s past. Learn about the Native American tribes that once roamed the area, explore the evolution of agriculture, or marvel at the intricate displays of local art.

3. Indulge in Retail Therapy at The Mall at Turtle Creek

Shopaholics rejoice! The Mall at Turtle Creek is a shopaholic’s paradise. With over 100 stores, including popular fashion brands, specialty shops, and delicious dining options, this mall is the perfect place to spend a day splurging on your favorite things. Take advantage of the sales and promotions, and treat yourself to a shopping spree.

4. Get Your Adrenaline Pumping at Jump! Zone

For a day filled with excitement and adventure, head to Jump! Zone. This indoor trampoline park offers a thrilling experience for both kids and adults. With a variety of bounce houses, trampolines, and inflatable slides, there’s no shortage of fun to be had. Release your inner child and bounce your way to a memorable day.

5. Unwind at Lake Frierson State Park

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and find tranquility at Lake Frierson State Park. This picturesque park is perfect for fishing, boating, and picnicking. Grab a fishing rod and try your luck at catching a big one, or simply relax by the lake and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

6. Discover History at the Parkin Archeological State Park

Travel back in time and uncover the history of Jonesboro at the Parkin Archeological State Park. This national historic landmark preserves the remains of a Native American village that existed over 500 years ago. Explore the exhibits and learn about the Mississippian culture that once thrived in this area.

7. Enjoy Family Fun at the Forrest L. Wood Crowley’s Ridge Nature Center

Immerse yourself in nature at the Forrest L. Wood Crowley’s Ridge Nature Center. This educational center offers interactive exhibits that showcase the unique flora and fauna of the region. Take a walk along the scenic trails, participate in a wildlife observation program, or attend one of their informative workshops.

8. Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth at Jones Bros. Cupcakes

Treat yourself to a delectable dessert at Jones Bros. Cupcakes. Known for their mouthwatering cupcakes in a variety of flavors, this local bakery is a must-visit for anyone with a sweet tooth. Indulge in their signature creations or try one of their seasonal specialties. Don’t forget to snap a picture for your Instagram feed!

9. Cheer on the Red Wolves at Centennial Bank Stadium

Experience the thrill of collegiate sports by attending a game at Centennial Bank Stadium. Home to the Arkansas State University Red Wolves football team, this stadium offers an electrifying atmosphere that is sure to get your heart pumping. Join in on the cheers and support the local team as they compete against rival schools.

10. Explore the Art Scene at the Bradbury Art Museum

Engage your senses and appreciate the artistic talents showcased at the Bradbury Art Museum. This contemporary art museum features rotating exhibitions that highlight a wide range of artistic styles and mediums. Discover emerging artists, attend a gallery talk, or participate in a hands-on workshop to unleash your own creativity.

As you can see, Jonesboro has an abundance of exciting activities to offer. Whether you’re seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply looking to indulge in some delicious treats, this vibrant city has got it all. So, pack your bags and get ready to enjoy the best of what Jonesboro has to offer.
