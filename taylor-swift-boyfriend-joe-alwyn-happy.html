Taylor Swift Is Reportedly &quot;Very Happy&quot; with Boyfriend Joe Alwyn

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Taylor Swift has been going strong with her man Joe Alwyn, although they have yet to take their relationship public, and she's reportedly "very happy."

Take a peek at three things to know about Alwyn—and his relationship with the "Look What You Made Me Do" superstar songstress.

1. Alwyn's acting career is on the rise ... and Swift is more than supportive

The British thespian recently wrapped his latest film, The Favourite, which stars Swift's longtime pal Emma Stone. While he was in the U.K. filming it (and Swift was laying low pre-album release) the songstress jetted across the pond to spend time with him for an "extended period of time," according to People. In addition to The Favourite, Alwyn is filming period drama Mary Queen of Scots, which stars Margot Robbie and Saoirse Ronan.

2. Swift's parents are fans of their relationship

Not only does Swift hop on a plane for Alwyn, he does the same for her. The mag reported that he visits her in the States often, is "getting to know her parents and everyone likes him." He is also very supportive of her work.

3. Don't look for Swift to post pics of Alwyn on the 'gram ... just yet

The duo has been keeping their relationship on the low, and "her decision to keep her relationship with Joe quiet is making her happy," a source told the mag. "They have been getting to know each other slowly without any pressure." The actor reportedly enjoys spending time at home with her. "They seem to be on the same page," the source continued.
